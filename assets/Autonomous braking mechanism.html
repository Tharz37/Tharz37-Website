The Mission Objective

During my tenure with Team Equinox (A-Baja SAE), myself and Rishikesh R were tasked with a high-stakes engineering challenge: a system capable of both autonomous and manual activation without failure. The primary obstacle was generating 1000N of force within an extremely restricted chassis envelope.

Evolution of Failure

Innovation is iterative. We benchmarked and simulated four traditional mechanisms before reaching our final build. Each iteration failed due to simulation constraints:

V1: BELL_CRANK CAD_VIEW
V2: 4-BAR_LINKAGE with pedal CAD_VIEW
V3: SCOTCH_YOKE CAD_VIEW

Simultaneous Victory

By returning to pure physics fundamentals, we engineered a Custom Pulley Assembly. This solution provided the required mechanical advantage while eliminating the vibration issues of the Slider-Crank and the torque-failure of the Bell Crank. This optimization directly contributed to the team securing AIR_7 & Kerala Rank 2nd.
